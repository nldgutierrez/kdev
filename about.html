    • About the guild

    Cafe Latte [KOHI] is a PH-based guild and home to anyone who wants to have fun, chill, and have a good time exploring content. KOHI is also home to those who believe in coffee supremacy. Enjoy your coffee~

    Vision

    Cafe Latte [KOHI] aspires to be an established guild known for running a wide variety of in-game content and making it fun. We aspire to leave a KOHI trademark that people will remember wherever they may be and serve as a go-to for all players looking to have fun.

    Values

    Inclusivity

    First and foremost, we believe that everyone should enjoy the game. We embrace diversity and respect all individuals, regardless of their background, gender, or gaming skills.

    Healthy

    Secondly, we promote healthy gaming. We understand that we all have a life outside the game and have actual responsibilities in the real world. Thus, we do not hold it against anyone when we are not available to play all the time.

    Integrity

    Last but not least, we uphold our moral principles and leave no space for any form of harassment, racism, discrimination, hate speech, and the like. We strive to ensure that the guild remains a sanctuary for everyone.

    History

    Founded by Sinaya.3096 on August 13, 2023, Cafe Latte [KOHI] started as nothing but a unique guild tag. KOHI came from the name of Sinaya.3096's cat, is the Japanese word for coffee; and Cafe Latte happens to be Sinaya.3096's favorite coffee.

    It was never intended to be an actual guild not until August 20, 2023, when the guild welcomed its first member. From then on, the guild gradually grew as it welcomed more players and explored new content as a group.

    Today, we have over a hundred members who share the same love for the game.
